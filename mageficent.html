MALEFICENT

Maleficent travels to a grand old castle to celebrate young Aurora's upcoming wedding to Prince Phillip. 
While there, she meets Aurora's future mother-in-law -- a conniving queen who hatches a devious plot to destroy the land's fairies. 
Hoping to stop her, Maleficent joins forces with a seasoned warrior and a group of outcasts to battle the queen and her powerful army.
